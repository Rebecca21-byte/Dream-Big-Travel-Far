Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at Five Jumeirah Village

You're eligible for a Genius discount at Five Jumeirah Village! To save at this property, all you have to do is sign in.
Situated in Dubai, 8 km from Dubai Autodrome, Five Jumeirah Village features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. With a bar, the 5-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. At the hotel, rooms include a wardrobe and a flat-screen TV.
Five Jumeirah Village offers a continental or buffet breakfast. The wellness area at the accommodation is comprised of a hot tub and a sauna.
The accommodation also provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests. Mall of the Emirates is 10 km from Five Jumeirah Village, while Burj Al Arab Tower is 14 km from the property. The nearest airport is Dubai International Airport, 32 km from the hotel. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.7 for a two-person trip.
